Arc Builds


These builds run on the arc subclass and include: The Flash's Fist and The Hammer of Dawn


The Flash's Fist

Subclass: Top Tree / Code of the Earthshaker



Exotic: An Insurmountable Skullfort

Stats: Melee: 100 | Thats it. Spec whatever else you want but melee 100

This build is centered around one thing and one thing only: Seismic Strike. The bottom tree arc is the most "optimized" for PvP aka: most cringe, but this build changes that. The exotic keeps your melee ability going by charging it upon kills AND you heal when you kill as well. All you should be doing is dashing around the map spamming the melee ability. If you see someone, zig-zag their bullets and dash into them smashing their dumb little face in. In PvE this is also fun to constantly be clearing whordes of enemies in one or two punches using the melee ability. Pre-emptively melee strike when turning corners or entering tight spaces and you'll be suprised how often you just hit it.

This build is by far the most fun out of all of them. Smashing everyone with constant seismic strikes causes a suprising amount of triggering on the opposing side, causing all team synergy to just fall apart and the enemies to hard target you, leaving your team wide open for a lot of free kills. PvE is fun as well, running head on into a mass of enemies and watching them disintigrate as you smash into them.

The Hammer of Dawn (PvE Exclusive)

Subclass: Middle Tree / Code of the Missile



Exotic: Cuirass of the Falling Star

Stats: Intellect: 100+ | Put strength at 100 for the kicks and giggles of ballistic slam if you want, otherwise spec recovery and/or resilience

Titan see boss, titan press super key, boss gone.

This class makes you the Hammer of Dawn from Gears of War. The point is to blast bosses and mini bosses sky high with a super powerful thundercrash super. All mods should be focused on getting you that super as often as possible to keep you moving through the levels minibosses to the boss where you let it rip. Simple, effective, and most importantly: FUN.
