About

Installation of the mod onto your Ghost is easy! Enter your character and press 'S' to scroll down to your appearences. Click your ghost to bring up the ghost mods menu and apply the new "Photography Ghost Mod" into your activities slot. Keep in mind that you will have to have a masterworked ghost to install. Once installed, your memories will be saved in the "Memories" file in your vault.
